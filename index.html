QNA from Clients

One of the most important features that a convention center should have is spaciousness. This allows the staging of different kinds of events allowing to maximize the space and layout; where exhibitors can set up trade booths to display their products, services, and other offerings.

A convention center should also be highly accessible. Event participants, guests, and keynote speakers must be able to reach the venue easily either by public or private transportation.

Another must-have feature of a convention center is its sound reputation. Though one may imagine that reputation may be difficult to measure, it is as easy as looking into its foreign and local affiliations more than the number of conventions and exhibitions or other events that are held in the venue year after year.

A convention center should be able to hold large-scale events. But it should also have the capacity to cater to organizations that may want to hold meetings in smaller scale or in more optimized locations. The availability of a variety of function rooms allows these corporations to better plan out events such as company meetings and company-wide celebrations. Others can also make use of the different function rooms to hold birthday parties, as long as they’re able to properly coordinate with the venue.
